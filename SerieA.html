Serie A - Italy

Serie A is the top professional football league in Italy, renowned for its tactical battles and skillful players. With clubs like Juventus, AC Milan, and Inter Milan, Serie A boasts a rich history and fierce competition.

  • Founded: 1898
  • Number of Teams: 20
  • Current Champions: Inter Milan
  • Most Titles: Juventus (36 titles)
