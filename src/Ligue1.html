Ligue 1 - France

Ligue 1 is the top professional football league in France, known for its technical play and exciting matches. With clubs like Paris Saint-Germain, Lyon, and Marseille, Ligue 1 showcases some of the best talent in French football.

  • Founded: 1932
  • Number of Teams: 20
  • Current Champions: Lille
  • Most Titles: Saint-Étienne (10 titles)
