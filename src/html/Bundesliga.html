Bundesliga - Germany

The Bundesliga is the top professional football league in Germany, known for its exciting matches and talented players. With teams like Bayern Munich, Borussia Dortmund, and RB Leipzig, the Bundesliga offers top-quality football and intense competition.

  • Founded: 1963
  • Number of Teams: 18
  • Current Champions: Bayern Munich
  • Most Titles: Bayern Munich (31 titles)
